## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Core Personality
The Bottom Feeder brand voice adapts based on context while maintaining authenticity:

**Professional** (for specs, comparisons, technical content)
- Expert but approachable
- Data-driven claims with evidence
- Clear, confident language

**Friendly** (for social media, community engagement)
- Conversational and warm
- Uses humor appropriately
- Celebrates customer wins

**Bold** (for promotional content, differentiators)
- Confident claims about quality
- Challenges the status quo
- "No hoses. No cords. No hassle."

### Key Messaging Pillars
1. **Freedom** - Cordless means no tangled hoses, no cord management
2. **Professional Quality** - Built for daily commercial use, perfect for home
3. **Time Savings** - Quick deployment, efficient cleaning, easy storage
4. **American Service** - US-based support, real people, real help

### Phrases to Use
- "No hoses. No cords. No hassle."
- "Pool cleaning freedom"
- "From pool to storage in minutes"
- "Professional-grade performance"
- "Cordless power, corded results"

### Phrases to Avoid
- Overpromising on battery life (be accurate: 1.5 hours)
- Disparaging competitors directly
- Technical jargon without explanation
- Pressure tactics or urgency manipulation

## Platform Strategy (Hootsuite Integration)

- **Best Performing:** Before/after transformations, satisfying cleaning videos, quick tips
- **Hashtags:** #PoolCleaning #CordlessPoolVacuum #PoolMaintenance #TheBottomFeeder #PoolLife #SatisfyingCleaning

- **Frequency:** 5-7x/week (can repost Reels with adjustments)
- **Content Mix:** Trending sounds, POV content, satisfying cleaning

- **Content Mix:** Long-form posts, testimonials, community engagement

### Content Repurposing Workflow
1. **Start with long-form** (blog post or YouTube video)
2. **Extract key points** for Twitter/X thread
3. **Create carousel** for Instagram/LinkedIn
4. **Cut to clips** for Reels/TikTok/Shorts
5. **Pull quotes** for static posts
6. **Summarize** for email newsletter

### Hootsuite Workflow
1. **Batch create** content weekly (Mondays)
2. **Schedule** through Hootsuite for optimal times
3. **Monitor** engagement daily
4. **Respond** to comments within 24 hours
5. **Analyze** weekly performance
6. **Adjust** strategy based on data

## Competitor Intelligence

### Primary Competitors
| Brand | Model | Price | Key Differentiator |
|-------|-------|-------|-------------------|
| **Dolphin** | Liberty 400 | $1,999 | Smart navigation, app control |
| **Polaris** | Freedom | $1,599 | Vortex vacuum technology |
| **Beatbot** | AquaSense | $1,499 | AI-powered, camera navigation |
| **Aiper** | Scuba S1 | $1,299 | Budget-friendly cordless |

### Our Differentiators
1. **Professional-grade build** - Not consumer plastic
2. **US-based support** - Real humans, real help
3. **Proven reliability** - 4.9★ rating
4. **Simple operation** - No app required, works immediately
5. **Compact storage** - 18 lbs, easy to carry and store

### Content Angles Against Competitors
- **vs. Dolphin:** More affordable, simpler to use, no subscription needed
- **vs. Polaris:** Lighter weight, easier storage, comparable power
- **vs. Beatbot:** Proven reliability, established brand, US support
- **vs. Aiper:** Professional vs. consumer grade, better build quality

### Common Objections to Address

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Battery life seems short" | 1.5 hours covers most residential pools; extra batteries available |
| "Is it as powerful as corded?" | Same suction performance without the hassle |
| "Manual retrieval is inconvenient" | Takes 30 seconds; part of engaged pool ownership |
| "Battery safety concerns" | Premium lithium cells with safety certifications |
| "Price is high" | Investment in quality; compare to lifetime of hose replacements |
